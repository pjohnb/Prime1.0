    Glossary

    Pre-Market · 08:00 – 09:29 ET

    08:00–08:30Check AI Briefing headline. Note concentration warnings and any DK NULLIFYING flags on open positions.
    08:30–09:00Review AI Position Advisor. Action any TRIM or EXIT recommendations before the open. Set time stops on positions approaching max hold.
    09:00–09:29Review overnight signals in the Signals tab. Note STRONG candidates and their DK states. Confirm Schwab connected + PAPER mode. Set Max Trades.

    Market Open · 09:30 – 10:30 ET

    09:30–09:45Watch for early UOA call surges — first 15 min often has the day's strongest UOA activity. Do not chase gapped-up signals.
    09:45–10:30Enter STRONG-tier signals with confirmed DK state. Check borrow on any SHORT candidates. Log entries.

    Mid-Day · 10:30 – 14:00 ET

    10:30–12:00Monitor open positions. Watch for DK state changes — a CONFIRMING shifting to NULLIFYING is a trim signal.
    12:00–14:00Review PEAD signals for post-earnings drift candidates. Avoid new entries after 13:30 unless STRONG + CONFIRMING.

    Close · 14:00 – 16:00 ET

    14:00–15:00Tighten stops on positions approaching the 5-day hold limit. Close positions that have hit their profit target.
    15:00–15:45Close any SHORT positions you do not want to carry overnight. Institutional order flow typically picks up in the last 45 min.
    15:45–16:00Final review of open positions. Confirm stops are set. Log the day's outcomes for ML capture.

    STRONG

    • Both primary triggers fired (e.g. UOA_CALL + PEAD_BEAT) OR single trigger with very high conviction score.
    • All technical confirmations pass.
    • DK state is CONFIRMING or NEUTRAL (CONFIRMING elevates a WATCH to STRONG automatically).
    • Action: full-size entry at your configured max position size.

    WATCH

    • One primary trigger fired AND technical confirmation passes.
    • DK state is NEUTRAL (no institutional dark-pool signal either way).
    • The candidate is valid but lower conviction — institutional backing is absent.
    • Action: reduced size (50% of normal) or wait for DK CONFIRMING before entry.

    SUPPRESSED

    • Technical signal and trigger are valid, but DK NULLIFYING overrides — institutional selling directly opposes the trade.
    • Action: skip. Do not trade SUPPRESSED signals. If you are already in a position that becomes SUPPRESSED, tighten the stop immediately.

    trigger_source reference

    • UOA_CALL — unusual call volume surge (bullish). Initiates PSA long candidates.
    • UOA_PUT — unusual put volume surge (bearish). Initiates SHORT candidates.
    • PEAD_BEAT — earnings beat (EPS surprise > 0). Initiates PSA + PEAD long candidates.
    • PEAD_MISS — earnings miss + guidance cut. Initiates PEAD + SHORT candidates.
    • NONE — technical-only (no predictive trigger). PSA stays WATCH; SHORT is rejected.

    DK Three-State Modifier

    Dark-pool (off-exchange) prints reveal institutional intent before it moves the public price. PRIME classifies every symbol's DK activity into one of three states and propagates it onto all non-DK signals for that symbol.

    CONFIRM
    CONFIRMING — Institutional buying aligns with signal direction
    • Long signal: auto-upgrade WATCH → STRONG; if already APPROVED, dk_confirming flag added for AI ranker.
    • Short signal: danger — institutional buying opposes the short thesis → signal blocked (dk_blocked).
    • Open position: lean HOLD; institutional money is with you.
    NEUTRAL
    NEUTRAL — No significant dark-pool activity
    • Signal passes through unchanged — no upgrade, no suppression.
    • Trade on the technical merit and trigger quality alone.
    • Open position: standard management rules apply.
    NULLIFY
    NULLIFYING — Institutional selling opposes signal direction
    • Long signal: suppressed (approval_status = SUPPRESSED). Do not enter.
    • Short signal: confirms — institutional selling aligns with short thesis → WATCH upgraded to STRONG.
    • Open long position: tighten stop immediately; consider TRIM or EXIT.

    dk_conviction

    The conviction score (0.0–1.0) behind the CONFIRMING or NULLIFYING verdict. A score of 0.8+ indicates very decisive institutional data. NULLIFYING rows floor at 0.5 — any hard NULLIFYING override is high-conviction by construction. NEUTRAL rows have no conviction score (null).

    Stop Rules

    • Long positions: –5% from entry price. Hard stop — no exceptions.
    • Short positions: +5% from entry price. Hard stop — no exceptions.
    • Index ETF (IDX): –3% (lower realized volatility).
    • Time stop: maximum 5-session hold. Close before day 6 regardless of P&L.

    Position Sizing

    • STRONG tier: full configured max position size.
    • WATCH tier: 50% of max (reduced conviction).
    • SHORT strategy: always 50% of normal size (additional short-side rule).
    • dk_conviction ≥ 0.8 + CONFIRMING: consider full size even on WATCH.

    Account Routing (MATA)

    • Short selling: not permitted in IRA-type accounts. MATA routes to margin/taxable only.
    • Options strategies: IRA excluded. Check MATA profile before placing any options trade.
    • Intraday (PSA/MTS): can route to any account type.

    Short-Selling Additional Rules

    • RTH only: entries restricted to 09:30–16:00 ET Mon–Fri.
    • Borrow required: Schwab locate must confirm borrowable before signal is written.
    • DK CONFIRMING = hard block: never short into institutional accumulation.
    • DK NULLIFYING = confirmation: institutional selling aligns with your short thesis.

    AI Advisor Overrides

    • HOLD: maintain current position. Review again at close.
    • TRIM: reduce to 50% of current size. Lock in partial gains.
    • EXIT: close the full position immediately. High urgency — institutional flow or risk factor present.
    • UNAVAILABLE: AI advisory offline. Apply standard stop and time-stop rules manually.
